About Elemium 466

Ei466 library is designed to work seamlessly with popular Python libraries like Pandas, NumpPy, Scikit-learn, Darts, and Backtrader, allowing users to leverage the power of these libraries for market data manipulation, analysis, and modeling.

By providing a specialized machine learning and market data analysis framework, Elemium 466 aims at simplifying the process of generating acurate, performant and robust algorithmic strategies with special emphasis on endogenous phenomena.
